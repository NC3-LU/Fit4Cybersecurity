Description

This self-assessment tool called Fit4Privacy is aimed to help you understand to what extent your organisation respects data protection requirements. In this version of the tool, the baseline is considered to be the General Data Protection Regulation (EU 2016/679). 


During this self-assessment, $$qnumber$$ multiple choice questions were asked. After analysing your answers, below you can find the recommendations suggested.

You can download this document in French, English or German. Like this, it will help you communicate the results to your colleagues, management or third-parties if necessary. Remember that GDPR is about accountability: your company is expected to put measures in place to be able to demonstrate it is respecting GDPR, and that these measure are effective.
