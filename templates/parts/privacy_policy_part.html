{{ _('Information regarding the processing of personal data') }}

{% blocktranslate trimmed %} This questionnaire is anonymous and does not require the collection of personal data from the people who replies to it.

Users are invited to answer this questionnaire, taking care not to communicate, in the free-text fields of it, any information allowing the identification of a natural person (e.g. surname, first name, contact details, etc.). In the event that personal data is however entered in the free-text fields of the online questionnaire, these will be deleted.

The answers to the online questionnaire will be accessible only by the Commissioner and the CNPD agents as well as by the LHC-NC3 agents dedicated to the ALTO project, in order to ensure the analysis of the answers given and the proper technical operation.

For any questions regarding the processing of personal data by the CNPD, you can contact the CNPD's Data Protection Officer (DPO): dpo@cnpd.lu {% endblocktranslate %}
